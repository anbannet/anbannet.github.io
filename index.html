Biography

I have received my Bachelor's, Master's, and Ph.D. degrees in Mathematics and Computer Science, Wuhan University of Technology (WUT), China, in 2010, 2012, and 2015, respectively. I am currently working as a Computational Scientist in the Department of Computer Science, Xavier University of Louisiana (XULA), New Orleans, United States.

My research interests include image processing, machine learning, data mining, online learning, convex/non-convex optimization, and deep learning. I have served as ad hoc reviewers for international journals including Technology in Cancer Research & Treatment, Complexity, Information Sciences, Neurocomputing, Cognitive Computation, IEEE Transactions on Medical Imaging, ACM Transactions on Knowledge Discovery from Data, Frontiers in Oncology, and Briefings in Bioinformatics; and international conferences such as ICDM'16, SDM'17, ICDM'17, ICDM'18, AIKE'18, ICAIS’19, AAAI'19, CIKM'19, ICDM'19, AAAI'20, ICDM'20, and AAAI'21.

My co-authors include Prof. Kun Zhang (XULA), Prof. Zhide Fang (LSUHSC), Prof. Victor Sheng (TTU), Prof. Hongwen Deng (TU), Prof. Chindo Hicks (LSUHSC), Prof. Yongsheng Gao (GU), Prof. Zhixiang Fang (WU), Prof. Qin Zou (WU), and etc.
